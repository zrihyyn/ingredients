Maltodextrin

9050-36-6

Synonyms or siblings

Dextrin, Dextrin type Ⅰ, Ⅱ, Ⅲ, Ⅳ, Dextrin (white), Dextrin 10

Funtion

Appeal - Flavor Enhancer, Process and Prep - Emulsifier, Stabilizer, Thickening Agent

Description

Maltodextrin, a strong thickener similar to cornstarch, is made from starch slurry close to corn syrup consistency. It's derived from wheat, rice, potatoes, or barley, with most proteins removed during processing. Beyond thickening, it replaces fat, enhances flavors in chocolate and candy, and is used in sugar substitutes and nutritional beverages. It's prized for its texture-enhancing properties in beer brewing and is popular among bodybuilders for its carb content.
