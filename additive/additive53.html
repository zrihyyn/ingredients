Propylene glycol

57-55-6

Synonyms or siblings

Propylene gylcol, a-Propylene glycol, 1,2-Propanediol, 1,2-Dihydroxypropane

Funtion

Preservative - Antioxidant, Process and Prep - Moisture Control Agent, Powder Flow Agent, Stabilizer, Thickening Agent

Description

Propylene glycol (PG) is a colorless, flavorless compound crucial for dispersing artificial colors and flavors in food. It also serves as a humectant and emulsifier in products like cosmetics and medicines. Widely used in industrial applications such as deicers and ink solvents, PG is biodegradable and considered safe in low concentrations due to its rapid metabolism in the body.
