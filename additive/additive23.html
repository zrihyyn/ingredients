Modified cornstarch

CAS number: Various

Synonyms or siblings

Food starch, Acid-treated starch, Bleached starch, Enzyme treated starch, Monostarch phosphate, Hydroxyproply starch, Distarch glycerol, Acetylated oxidized starch, Pregel

Funtion

Process and Prep - Moisture Control Agent, Thickening Agent

Description

In the 1960s, food processors developed pregelatinized starch, known as "pregel," which thickens without cooking and retains its thickness. Made by chemically altering cornstarch with propylene oxide and phosphorus oxychloride, it improves texture in beverages and microwave meals, mimicking the mouthfeel of fats and eggs in desserts and sauces without adding calories. It's used for moisture control in creamy fillings and requires no cooking.

설명

변성전분은 물리적, 화학적, 생물학적 방법을 통해 전분 입자의 구조를 변화시켜 원래 전분이 갖지 않는 특성을 부여하는 전분의 일종이다. 식품의 점착성 및 점도를 증가시키고 유화안정성을 증진하며 식품의 물성 및 촉감을 향상시키는 등의 다양한 기능을 하며, 주로 식품에 증점제로 사용된다.
