Description

Whey, once a cheese-making byproduct, is now a valuable ingredient known for enhancing flavor and browning in baked goods. It's widely used in processed foods to bind water, maintain freshness, and improve texture. Whey's proteins are crucial in creating stable foams for cakes and toppings. It's also popular in dairy products, processed meats, and nutritional supplements due to its nutritional benefits. Additionally, whey derivatives are used in personal care products for their antimicrobial properties.

설명

유청은 우유를 치즈로 가공할 때 형성되는 부산물로, 치즈의 종류와 가공 과정에 따라서 크게 2가지로 분류된다. 우리가 흔히 유청이라고 하면 대부분 Sweet whey(단유청)을 가리키며 이는 이유식, 탄산음료, 리코타 치즈 그리고 각종 건강식품 등 다양한 식품에 첨가물로 사용된다.
